# Shop Owner Demo Script

## Pitch
Your customers scan QR, order from their phone, and your staff gets a clean packing queue. No paper confusion, no shouting tokens, no Swiggy/Zomato commission.

## Demo Flow
1. Show QR customer page.
2. Enter customer name.
3. Search item.
4. Add 2-3 items.
5. Place cash order.
6. Show picker dashboard receiving order instantly.
7. Press sound on.
8. Confirm cash and pack.
9. Mark ready.
10. Show queue display token.
11. Hand over order.
12. Show owner dashboard revenue and CSV export.

## Pricing Talk
- Setup: Rs. 2,000 to Rs. 5,000
- Maintenance: Rs. 999 to Rs. 1,999 per month
- Delivery module later: Rs. 2,999 per month
- QR stands/tablet charged separately
